Gake no Ue no Ponyo

Gake no Ue no Ponyo (崖の上のポニョ, がけのうえのポニョ) can be shortened to Ponyo, is an anime film made by Studio Ghibli that premiered in 2008, Miyazaki Hayao directed and wrote the script for movie. This is the eighth film Miyazaki has made for Ghibli and his tenth. The plot revolves around a goldfish girl named Polumisharudin who wants to become a human, she befriends a five-year-old boy named Sōsuke and he names him Ponyo.

The film won many different awards, including the award for the best animated film of the year by the Japanese Academy. The film was released in Japan on July 19, 2008, in North America on August 14, 2009, and in the UK on February 12, 2010. The film ranked at number nine for the week's highest-grossing films. premiered in the United States.
